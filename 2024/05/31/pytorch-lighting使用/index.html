CoreAPI

LightningModule

一个module主要是六部分组成

  • Initialization (__init__ and setup()).
  • Train Loop (training_step())
  • Validation Loop (validation_step())
  • Test Loop (test_step())
  • Prediction Loop (predict_step())
  • Optimizers and LR Schedulers (configure_optimizers())
